What This Analytics Delivers

This platform converts CricClubs pages, scorecards, statistics tables, and commentary into a structured player intelligence layer for Bay Area U15 cricket. It allows selectors and coaches to see not only who produced numbers, but who produced them against strong opponents, in difficult match situations, and against quality individual players.

It supports player search, comparison, ranking, and report generation with a clear explanation of why a player is being rated highly or flagged for further attention.

Why This Is Different

Pressure creation Matchup intelligence Quality of opposition Transparent scoring Development tracking

Two players can post similar scorecard totals and still have very different selector value. This system is designed to reveal that difference quickly.

Why Selectors Care

Not all 40s, 2-wicket spells, or tidy overs mean the same thing. This system adjusts performance by the quality of the environment in which it occurred. A batter who scores against a stronger attack in Div 1 Phase 2 deserves a different reading than a batter who scores similar runs in a weaker setting.

The same applies to bowlers. A wicket of a top-order elite batter should count differently from a wicket against a struggling lower-order player. Opponent-adjusted analytics makes that visible.

How The Stats Are Built

1. Discover

Start from a single series page, identify U15 target divisions, and detect stats, results, scorecard, and commentary routes.

2. Capture

Save raw HTML and structured response data so every derived metric remains traceable and reprocessable.

3. Normalize

Convert raw pages into structured match, innings, player, fielding, and ball-event entities.

4. Reconcile

Verify commentary totals, wickets, batting cards, and bowling figures against official scorecards.

5. Compute

Create ball-by-ball metrics, opponent-adjusted metrics, matchup views, and development signals.

6. Publish

Deliver selector dashboards, peer comparisons, downloadable reports, and player-level drill-down views.
